Privacy

How On The Run Fit handles your information.

Simple version: information you submit is used to reply to your coaching or program inquiry. If analytics or advertising tools are used, they are disclosed here.

Information collected

If you submit an application, On The Run Fit may collect your name, email, running event, plan interest, goals, and any details you choose to share.

How it is used

That information is used to respond to your inquiry, recommend the right coaching or program option, and provide requested follow-up.

Email

If you opt into updates, you may receive occasional coaching or program emails. Marketing emails should include a way to unsubscribe.

Analytics and ads

Tools such as Google Analytics or Meta Pixel may use cookies or similar technologies to understand site traffic and improve follow-up.

Service providers

Form, email, checkout, analytics, and ad tools may process information on behalf of On The Run Fit once connected.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to ontherunfit@gmail.com.
