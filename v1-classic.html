Why A Coach

You're not lazy. You're just doing it alone.

I've been there — comebacks, busy seasons, all of it. Here's what trying to figure it out solo tends to cost us.

🩹

Injuries that steal seasons

Random mileage and skipping strength work is the fastest way to the sidelines, friend. One injury can wipe out months of work — and the race you were so excited for.

🔁

A plan that isn't really yours

A free plan off the internet doesn't know your body, your history, or your crazy schedule. So you work SO hard on the wrong things and wonder why the PRs stopped.

💕

Nobody cheering you on

When motivation dips or life gets busy, there's no one tweaking the plan, keeping you accountable, or telling you you're ready. That's the lonely part — and where most of us quit.

You've already got the discipline to lace up. What you're missing is a plan that's actually yours — and someone genuinely in your corner. That's where I come in. 🔥

Meet Your Coach

Hi, I'm Lindsey! 👋

USATF Level 1 Certified Coach · 400m Masters Track Athlete · Boy Mom · Believer · Wife

I've been a runner since junior high — all through high school, four years of track and cross country at Texas State, and now as a USA Masters track athlete still chasing PRs in my main event, the 400m. FAQ

Good questions

Do I have to be an experienced runner?

No. I coach everyone from first-time 5K runners and middle-school athletes to masters runners chasing lifetime PRs. Your plan is built for exactly where you are right now.

I'm a sprinter / I run track — can you help?

Absolutely! I coach the full range — sprints, middle distance, and long distance, from 100m all the way to the marathon. Your training is built specifically for your event, not some one-size-fits-all distance plan.

How is the coaching delivered?

Online athletes get their plan through a coaching app, plus check-ins and messaging based on your tier. In-person coaching is available locally in New Braunfels, TX.

What if I get injured or my schedule blows up?

That's exactly why you have a coach. I adjust your plan as life happens — travel, illness, busy weeks, or niggles — so you keep progressing safely instead of starting over.

Is it worth it if I could find a free plan online?

I get the question! But a free plan can't see your body, your history, or your crazy week — so you end up working SO hard on the wrong things. Coaching is the difference between just running and actually training toward something. And honestly? It costs less a day than your coffee. 💕

Which plan should I choose?

If you're not sure, just apply below and I'll help you figure it out! I'll look at your goals and point you to the plan that fits your life and your budget — zero pressure.
