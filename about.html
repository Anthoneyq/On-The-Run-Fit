Why Lindsey

A speed coach who understands the whole runner.

Her own event is the 400m, but her coaching range runs from first-time 5Ks to marathon goals, track athletes, cross country runners, masters athletes, and comeback runners.

That speed background matters. Distance runners often need mechanics, strength, turnover, and confidence just as much as mileage. Lindsey brings those pieces together without making the runner feel like a template.

Coaching philosophy

Build the plan around the human.

Specific beats generic

The plan should match the runner's event, training history, body, schedule, and real life.

Confidence is trained

Pacing, check-ins, form work, and race plans reduce guessing and build belief.

Durability matters

Strength, mobility, and recovery are not extras. They are how runners keep showing up.
